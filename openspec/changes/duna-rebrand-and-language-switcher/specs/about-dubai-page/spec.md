## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: About Dubai Prime Locations heading

The About Dubai "Prime Locations" section SHALL present "Prime Locations" as a single prominent heading at the primary section heading size (`text-4xl md:text-5xl`). The former "Key Neighborhoods & Areas" subtitle SHALL be removed.

#### Scenario: Single enlarged Prime Locations heading

- **WHEN** a visitor views the About Dubai neighborhoods section
- **THEN** "Prime Locations" is shown as the large section heading
- **AND** the "Key Neighborhoods & Areas" subtitle is not present
