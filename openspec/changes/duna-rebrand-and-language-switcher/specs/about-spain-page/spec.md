### Requirement: About Spain Prime Locations heading

The About Spain "Prime Locations" section SHALL present "Prime Locations" as a single prominent heading at the primary section heading size (`text-4xl md:text-5xl`). The former "Key neighborhoods & areas" subtitle SHALL be removed.

#### Scenario: Single enlarged Prime Locations heading

- **WHEN** a visitor views the About Spain neighborhoods section
- **THEN** "Prime Locations" is shown as the large section heading
- **AND** the "Key neighborhoods & areas" subtitle is not present
